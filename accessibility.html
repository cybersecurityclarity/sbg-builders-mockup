Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

Our Commitment

SBG Builders is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to ensure we provide equal access to all our users.

Standards and Conformance

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA standards published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and user-friendly for everyone.

Measures to Support Accessibility

SBG Builders takes the following measures to ensure accessibility of our website:

  • Include accessibility as part of our mission statement
  • Integrate accessibility into our procurement practices
  • Provide continual accessibility training for our staff
  • Include people with disabilities in our design personas
  • Employ formal accessibility quality assurance methods

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Keyboard Navigation: All functionality is available using keyboard controls
  • Screen Reader Compatibility: Content is structured for screen reader users
  • Text Alternatives: Images include descriptive alt text
  • Color Contrast: Sufficient contrast ratios throughout the site
  • Resizable Text: Text can be resized up to 200% without loss of content or functionality
  • Clear Navigation: Consistent navigation structure across all pages
  • Forms: Clearly labeled form fields with error identification
  • Skip Links: Skip navigation links for keyboard users

Technical Specifications

Accessibility of our website relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • WAI-ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications)

Limitations and Alternatives

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility, there may be some limitations. If you encounter accessibility barriers on our website, please let us know so we can work to resolve the issue.

Assessment Approach

SBG Builders assessed the accessibility of our website by the following approaches:

  • Self-evaluation
  • External evaluation by accessibility consultants
  • Automated testing tools
  • Manual testing with assistive technologies

Browser and Assistive Technology Compatibility

Our website is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Recent versions of popular screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
  • Speech recognition software
  • Screen magnification software
  • Browser accessibility features

Our website is compatible with:

  • Recent versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge
  • Mobile browsers on iOS and Android devices

Formal Complaints

If you are not satisfied with our response to your accessibility concern, you may contact the appropriate regulatory authority in your jurisdiction.

Ongoing Improvements

We are continuously working to improve the accessibility of our website. As we implement new features and content, we will continue to review and improve accessibility. This accessibility statement will be updated as needed to reflect these efforts.
