Accessibility Statement

SBG Builders is committed to ensuring our digital experiences are usable by everyone.

SBG Builders (“we,” “our,” or the “Company”) is committed to diversity, inclusion, and accessibility. These values are central to how we design, develop, and deliver our digital content and services, including our website(s), applications, and other online tools (collectively, the “Digital Properties”). We strive to ensure that our Digital Properties are accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities.

Commitment to Accessibility

We are actively working to make our Digital Properties accessible and usable by people of all abilities and disabilities. To achieve this, we follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA as our standard for accessibility. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with visual, auditory, cognitive, and motor impairments.

Standards and Ongoing Efforts

We are continuously working to improve accessibility features and regularly review and update our Digital Properties to enhance usability. Efforts may include:

  • Designing new content in accordance with recognized accessibility standards.
  • Conducting periodic reviews and testing for accessibility compliance.
  • Incorporating user feedback and accessibility best practices into ongoing development.

Despite these efforts, portions of our Digital Properties may not yet meet all accessibility standards. Some content or third-party features may be temporarily inaccessible to some users, and we are committed to addressing these areas over time.

Known Limitations

While we work to make our entire Digital Properties accessible, there are situations where full conformance may not yet be achievable:

  • Some third-party content or widgets not fully controlled by SBG Builders could have accessibility barriers.
  • Archived content or older pages may not meet current accessibility standards.
  • We aim to address accessibility issues as they are identified and prioritized.

Compatibility and Technical Support

Our Digital Properties are designed to be compatible with modern assistive technologies and devices. However, some combinations of browsers and assistive tools (such as screen readers or magnification software) might not be fully supported in all cases.

Feedback and Contact Information

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of SBG Builders’ Digital Properties. If you encounter accessibility barriers or have suggestions to improve access, please contact us:

Email: info@sbgbuilders.com

When reporting an issue, please include:

  • The specific web page or feature where the issue occurred
  • The type of accessibility challenge you experienced
  • The device, browser, or assistive technology you were using

We will do our best to respond within a reasonable timeframe and work toward a solution.

Review and Updates

This Accessibility Statement is reviewed at least annually and updated whenever significant changes are made to our Digital Properties or accessibility practices.
