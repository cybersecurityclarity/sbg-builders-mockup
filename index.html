Services

Pre-Construction

SBG delivers comprehensive pre-construction services including preliminary budgeting, cost modeling, proforma analysis, and value engineering to establish financial feasibility and project viability. Through due diligence, constructability review, scope development, bid packaging, and detailed scheduling strategies, the team aligns design intent, cost control, and execution planning before mobilization.

During Construction

SBG provides full-service general contracting and design-build delivery with disciplined project controls, field supervision, and subcontractor procurement to maintain schedule and budget integrity. With rigorous QA/QC protocols, safety management, document control, and proactive change management, SBG mitigates risk while driving transparent, technology-enabled communication throughout construction.

Closeout & Beyond

SBG manages final inspections, punchlist completion, turnover coordination, and comprehensive closeout documentation including O and M manuals to ensure seamless project delivery. Post-completion, the team supports warranty administration, portfolio planning, and long-term capital strategy to position clients for sustained operational growth.
